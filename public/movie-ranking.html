The Best Movies According to Kris

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

This is my favourite trilogy of all-time. I love the cinematography, acting, costume and set design, special effects, EVERYTHING!!

The Matrix

Action packed philosophical, metaphysical and existential thriller. I've lost times how many times I've watched the first movie. (Just the first movie, I like to pretend that the others never happened.)

Jurassic Park

This 1993 movie started my obsession and love affair with all things dinosaurs and paleontology. The special effects and animatronics still hold up to this day.

Bonus: The Blues Brothers

Hilarious comedy, comically insane car chars, soulful soundtrack and many guests appearances make for an extremely entertaining and rewhatchable film.
