The Best Movies According to Kris

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

The Lord of the Rings (Extended Trilogy)

My favourite film trilogy of all-time. I love the cinematography, the acting, costume and set design, special effects, and most importantly the story. It is a timeless masterpiece.

The Matrix

A mind bending, philosophical, metaphysical and existential action packed thriller. I've watched and rewatched it many times.

Jurassic Park

This 1993 classic started my obsession and love affair with all things dinosaurs and paleontology. The special effects and animatronics still hold up incredibly well to this day.

Bonus: The Blues Brothers

A hilarious comedy featuring comically insane car chases, while boasting a soulful bluesy soundtrack and many with many special guests appearances make for an extremely entertaining and rewatchable film.
